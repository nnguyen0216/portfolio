Team: Jason Ding, Cody Smith, and Nancy Nguyen

Timeline: January - April, 2019

My role: UI/UX Design, Research Director, Front-End Development

The problem: Food waste is no stranger to college students. Oftentimes, hectic schedules get in the way, causing potholes in budgets and groceries left forgotten in the back of the fridge. This disrupts the student's budget and contributes to greenhouse gases from food waste.

The design challenge: How might we design a budgeting app that zones in on food waste for busy young professionals (aged 18-25)?

Our solution: An app designed with a minimalistic feel that will contrast with the young professional's hectic lifestyle.
