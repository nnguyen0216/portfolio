Flu Vaccination Promotions Project


No needles in sight.

Goal: To encourage UCSD students to get their flu shot through a warm + welcoming advert.

The Process: The UCSD Health Promotion Services (HPS) center wanted to advertise a free* flu vaccine clinic event to students on campus. This year, the HPS director requested an inviting social media post (last year's was not very welcoming as it contained needles) and granted me the role of creative director for this project.


Color played an important role in the welcoming aspect of this project. I chose warmer-toned pinks and nude tones in order to maintain a softer appearance, so that students would feel welcomed when encountering the post on their feeds. To garner attention, I chose a blue color for the *most* important information, ensuring that students wouldn't miss it.
